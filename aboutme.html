About Me

Hello, I’m Jordan! I’m a wife and mother of two amazing boys, and I’m starting my photography journey right here in beautiful San Diego, California. Photography has always been a passion of mine, and now I’m diving in headfirst, capturing the moments that matter most. Whether I’m photographing landscapes, family moments, or the little things that make life special, my goal is to tell stories through my lens. I’m excited to share this adventure with you and can’t wait to show you what I create!.
